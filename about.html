Welcome to Belades Global Concept

We are Belades Global Concept, a private limited liability company registered under the Company and Allied Matters Act, LFN 2004 and relevant laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria with the business of branding of promotional and corporate gift items industry.


We are driven by our mission and core values coupled with our passion and commitment to meet all your needs in the areas of corporate gifts and general branding, both indoor and outdoor etc.


Ours clients include corporate organizations, Educational Institutions, Religious Organization, Government parastatals and Agencies, Individual and Para-military bodies.


At Belades Global Concept, our main aim is your satisfaction. We therefore concern ourselves with offering you and your organization the best quality service(s) including but not limited to timely delivery. This is achieved with our state-of-the-art modern equipment and machineries as well as highly trained motivated and experienced personnel.


At Belades Global Concept, we are so optimistic that a trial will convince you.
